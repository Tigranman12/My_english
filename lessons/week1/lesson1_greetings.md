# Lesson 1 — Greetings & Introductions

> **Level:** Beginner | **Time:** 1 hour | **Week:** 1, Day 1

---

## 🎯 Goals
- Learn basic greetings
- Introduce yourself in English
- Ask and answer simple questions

---

## 1. Greetings

### Formal
| English           | When to use              |
|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Good morning      | Before 12:00 PM          |
| Good afternoon    | 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM       |
| Good evening      | After 6:00 PM            |
| Hello             | Any time, polite         |

### Informal
| English           | When to use              |
|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Hi                | Friends, casual          |
| Hey               | Close friends            |
| What's up?        | Very casual              |

---

## 2. Introducing Yourself

### Basic structure:
```
My name is [name].
I am [age] years old.
I am from [country/city].
I speak [language].
```

### Example:
> *"Hi! My name is Tigran. I am from Armenia. I am learning English."*

---

## 3. Common Questions & Answers

| Question                  | Answer                        |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------|
| What is your name?        | My name is ...                |
| Where are you from?       | I am from ...                 |
| How old are you?          | I am ... years old.           |
| How are you?              | I am fine, thank you.         |
| Nice to meet you.         | Nice to meet you too.         |
| Do you speak English?     | A little. I am learning.      |

---

## 4. Key Vocabulary

| Word         | Meaning         | Example                        |
|--------------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| name         | имя             | My name is Tigran.             |
| from         | из              | I am from Armenia.             |
| speak        | говорить        | I speak Armenian and Russian.  |
| learn        | учиться         | I am learning English.         |
| meet         | познакомиться   | Nice to meet you!              |
| please       | пожалуйста      | Can you help me, please?       |
| thank you    | спасибо         | Thank you very much!           |
| sorry        | извините        | Sorry, I don't understand.     |

---

## 5. Pronunciation Tips

- **"Th"** sound — put your tongue between your teeth:
  - *thank* → **th**ank
  - *the* → **th**e

- **"R"** in English is softer than in Russian/Armenian:
  - *from* — don't roll the R

## ✏️ Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1 — Fill in the blank
1. My _______ is Tigran.
2. I am _______ Armenia.
3. Nice to _______ you.
4. How _______ you?

### Exercise 2 — Write your introduction
Write 4–5 sentences about yourself:

```
Hi! My name is _______. I am _______ years old.
I am from _______. I speak _______.
I am learning English because _______.
```

### Exercise 3 — Match
| Question             | Answer                  |
|----------------------|-------------------------|
| How are you?         | I am from Armenia.      |
| What is your name?   | Nice to meet you too.   |
| Where are you from?  | My name is Tigran.      |
| Nice to meet you.    | I am fine, thank you.   |

---

## 📝 Summary

- Use **Hello / Hi** to greet
- Use **My name is...** to introduce yourself
- Use **I am from...** to say where you are from
- Always say **Nice to meet you** when meeting someone new
