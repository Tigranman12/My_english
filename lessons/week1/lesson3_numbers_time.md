## 3. Telling the Time

### Key phrases:
| Expression     | Meaning           | Example               |
|----------------|-------------------|-----------------------|
| o'clock        | ровно             | It's 3 o'clock.       |
| past           | после (минут)     | It's 10 past 3.       |
| to             | до (минут)        | It's 10 to 4.         |
| half past      | половина          | It's half past 2.     |
| quarter past   | четверть после    | It's quarter past 5.  |
| quarter to     | без четверти      | It's quarter to 6.    |

### Examples:
| Clock  | How to say              |
|--------|-------------------------|
| 3:00   | It's three o'clock.     |
| 3:15   | It's quarter past three.|
| 3:30   | It's half past three.   |
| 3:45   | It's quarter to four.   |
| 3:10   | It's ten past three.    |
| 3:50   | It's ten to four.       |

### Useful questions:
- **What time is it?** — Который час?
- **What time does it start?** — Во сколько начинается?

## 6. Dates

### How to say dates:
```
[Month] + [Ordinal number]
```

| Date       | How to say                    |
|------------|-------------------------------|
| 1st March  | March the first               |
| 15th June  | June the fifteenth            |
| 22nd March | March the twenty-second       |

## 📝 Summary

- Numbers 1–12 are unique, learn them by heart
- **Half past** = :30 | **Quarter past** = :15 | **Quarter to** = :45
- Days and months always start with a **capital letter**
- Dates use ordinal numbers: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th...
